## The Challenge

A mid-sized law firm with 25 attorneys was struggling to keep up with contract review volume. Their existing process involved:

- Manual review of each contract by junior associates
- 2-3 days average turnaround per contract
- Inconsistent extraction of key terms
- High risk of missed clauses or errors

With client demands increasing and talent scarce, they needed a better way.

## Our Approach

### Discovery Phase

We started with a 2-week discovery engagement to understand their specific workflows:

- Shadowed the contract review process
- Analyzed 200+ contracts to identify patterns
- Interviewed attorneys at all levels
- Mapped integration requirements with their document management system

### Key Insights

Through our analysis, we discovered:

1. **80% of clauses were standard** across contract types, making them ideal for AI extraction
2. **20% required human judgment** but could benefit from AI-powered suggestions
3. **Most time was spent searching** rather than analyzing

### Solution Design

We designed a hybrid AI system that:

- **Automatically extracts** standard clauses, dates, parties, and key terms
- **Flags unusual language** for human review
- **Integrates directly** with their existing document management system
- **Learns from corrections** to improve over time

## Implementation

The implementation took 8 weeks:

**Weeks 1-2: Data Pipeline**
- Connected to document management system
- Set up secure processing environment
- Configured extraction models

**Weeks 3-4: Model Training**
- Trained custom extraction models on their specific contract types
- Validated accuracy against manual review

**Weeks 5-6: Integration**
- Built user interface for review workflow
- Integrated with existing systems
- Set up reporting dashboard

**Weeks 7-8: Training & Launch**
- Trained staff on new workflow
- Ran parallel processing to validate
- Gradual rollout to all practice areas

## Key Takeaways

1. **Start with a focused use case.** We didn't try to automate everything—just the high-volume, pattern-based work.

2. **Hybrid AI + Human works best.** The AI handles extraction; humans handle judgment. Each does what they're best at.

3. **Integration matters.** The solution fits into their existing workflow rather than requiring new processes.

4. **Training is essential.** We invested heavily in training to ensure adoption.
